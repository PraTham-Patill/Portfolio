Best Movies According to Pratham

Top Most Exciting Movies

The Social Network (2010):

Directed by David Fincher, this film tells the story of the founding and rise of Facebook. It features a compelling script by Aaron Sorkin and received critical acclaim for its sharp dialogue, strong performances, and exploration of the impact of social media on society.

Parasite (2019):

Directed by Bong Joon-ho, "Parasite" is a South Korean black comedy thriller that gained international acclaim. The film explores social class dynamics and won several awards, including the Palme d'Or at the Cannes Film Festival and four Academy Awards, including Best Picture.

Mad Max: Fury Road (2015):

Directed by George Miller, "Mad Max: Fury Road" is an action-packed post-apocalyptic film that received widespread praise for its stunning visuals, practical effects, and intense storytelling. It was a critical and commercial success, earning multiple Academy Awards.
